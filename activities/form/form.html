1. The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? The action attribute tells the browser where to send the form data when the form is submitted.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? The method attribute tells the browser how to send the form data (GET or POST).

3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"? GET sends data through the URL, while POST sends data securely in the request body.
