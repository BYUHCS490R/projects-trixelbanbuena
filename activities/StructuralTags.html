Structural Tags

For future assignments, there must be a header, main, and footer

What goes in the header? The top part of the page; Usually has the site title, logo, or tagline.

What goes in Nav? The menu for moving around the site; Usually a list of links to pages or sections.

What goes in the Main? The main content of the page; Holds articles, imagaes, videos, etc.

What goes in the footer? The bottom of the page; Usually has copyright, contact info, social links, or small menus.

HTML validation means checking if our HTML code is correct. It helps catch errors like inclosed tags or wrong nesting.
