Box Model

questions

1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

answer:

Make elements that start on a new line and can be changed in size. Inline tags stay in the same line as the text and are used to style words, like making them bold or italic.

2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

answer:

The space outside the box, border is the edge of the box, and padding is the space inside the box between the content and the border.

3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

answer:

px, %, em to set sizes.

4. When would you use a figure tag?

answer:

Used to hold a picture or image and its caption that explains what it is.
