How Grassroots Began

  • Started inside the Empower Your Dreams (EYD) program at BYU–Hawaii.
  • Run by students who enjoy marketing, writing, design, and small business stories.
  • Built so local owners get simple marketing help and students gain real client experience.

“We learn by doing, and your business gets the benefit of that energy.”

About Grassroots by GPT

Grassroots by GPT is a student-led marketing studio that helps local businesses show up clearly online. We turn what you already do well into simple photos, captions, and brand pieces that feel like your voice.

We meet with you, listen to your goals, and walk through your shop or service. From there we outline a small plan and create starter content so your community can find and trust you online while you stay focused on running the business.

Meet the Grassroots Team

Grassroots by GPT is a small student team at BYU–Hawaii that cares about local businesses like yours. We visit your shop, listen to your story, and ask about the goals that actually matter to you, not just follower counts. Then we turn that into photos, posts, and words that sound like you. Our mix of writing, design, research, and planning keeps your marketing clear and consistent, without the cost or pressure of a big agency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make marketing feel manageable for small business owners. You do not need a huge campaign to move forward — you need honest photos, clear words,and a plan that fits into your week.

  • Keep everything simple and understandable, even if you are not comfortable with technology.
  • Offer thoughtful support to local businesses that are not ready for a big agency.
  • Give students real project work so they bring fresh ideas and current skills to each client.

Who We Are

Grassroots by GPT is powered by a small group of students from Business Management and related programs. Some focus on writing, some on visuals, and others on research and planning. For each client we build a small project team, so you always work with people who know your story.

This site shows where we are starting, not the final version. As we work with more clients, we will share more examples and refine our services. What will stay the same is our approach: listen carefully, respect your time, and treat your business like it matters — because it does.
