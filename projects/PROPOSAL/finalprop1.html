Grassroots by GPT

1. Title and Topic

This website will feature Grassroots by GPT, a business created by Empower Your Dreams (EYD) participants. Their business helps small local companies with marketing, social media content, and basic consulting.

2. Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this topic because it is connected to a company seeking to join Empower Your Dreams in Winter 2026. This site will contain information about the startup business idea of three students from the BUSM department. This site will also serve as a means for businesses that will procure the student’s services to communicate with the team.

3. Pages and Content I will include

  • Home Page – intro to Grassroots by GPT, mission, featured business, team
  • About Us – how the project started, goals, background of EYD
  • Services – marketing, content creation, branding, consulting
  • Business Intake Form – fields such as business name, challenges, and contact info
  • Client Table – a table showing sample businesses and status
  • Portfolio & Media – sample posts, reels, photos

4. What fields (or information you will collect) will you put into your form? What data will go on your table?

The form will collect the basic details needed by Grassroots by GPT to handle inquiries and orders. This includes the customer’s name, email, optional phone number, type of inquiry, message or order details, preferred contact method, selected product, and quantity.

The table will display the team members’ schedules of Geiri, Pak, and Taiel. It will show their names, locations, times, and the skills they will use during their assigned shifts, making it easy to track who is scheduled and where.

5. How will I collect information?

  1. Info directly from Grassroots by GPT team
  2. Their business notes and intake forms
  3. Pictures and videos they take
  4. Public info from their social media
  5. General marketing resources online
