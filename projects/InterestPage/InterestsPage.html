Interests

I’ve always been drawn to things that make my brain work a little harder. I enjoy brain teaser games like Sudoku and other logic puzzles. Thrillers and horror movies give me the same feeling; they are like living puzzles.Every detail feels like a clue, and I enjoy trying to solve the mystery before the ending. Watching these stories makes me feel like I’m part of the game.

Thrillers also remind me of my studies in AIT. Just like solving a puzzle, I have to search for patterns, analyze details, and find answers. I also love sewing and customizing clothes—it is my creative way of building characters. For me, thrillers are not just about fear; they are about focus, puzzles, and imagination.
